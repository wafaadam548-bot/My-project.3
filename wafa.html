Question Answere
What are the school hours? The school is open from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM.
When do the holidays start? Holidays begin at the end of each term.
Summer break runs from mid-June to the end of August.
What courses does WCI offer? We offer core subjects such as Math, Science, English, and History,
along with electives like Arts, Music, and Technology.
How can I register at WCI? You can fill out the registration form online
and submit the required documents (previous school transcript, ID, etc.).
Does WCI support students with special needs? Yes, WCI has a dedicated
support team that works with teachers to provide accommodations.
What extracurricular activities are available? We offer sports teams, music groups, science clubs,
theater, and cultural activities.
How can I check my child’s grades? Grades are available on the school’s
online portal or in midterm and final report cards.
What happens if I arrive late to class? You must report to the office, and the late arrival will be recorded.
You may also need to make up missed work.
Is there a school uniform? Yes, students are expected to follow the school’s dress code.
Details are available in the student handbook.
How do I contact a teacher or the administration? You can send an email,
use the contact form on the website, or visit the office directly.
What is the attendance policy? Absences must be excused with a written or medical note.
Excessive absences may lead to academic consequences.
Does the school provide meals? Yes, the cafeteria serves meals at affordable prices.
Menus are posted weekly.
Can parents volunteer at the school? Yes, parents are welcome to volunteer in school events.
Please contact the administration or Parent-Teacher Association.
How can I book school facilities after hours? Contact the office or
facilities department to request booking of gyms or halls in advance.
